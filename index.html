About the ISS

The International Space Station is a modular space station in low Earth orbit. It's the largest artificial object in space and can often be seen with the naked eye from Earth.

It orbits Earth approximately every 92 minutes at 27,600 km/h, completing about 15.5 orbits per day at roughly 408 km altitude.
